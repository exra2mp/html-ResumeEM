Task and functions for:

  • Plant and Equipment Manager:

    • Supervision of Installation and assembly of the production plant.
    • Technical calculations of the mass flows for the start-up.
    • Coordinator of asphalt emulsions production plant, equipment operator and design of asphalt emulsion formulations for field application.
    • Quality controls of the emulsions produced by analyzing asphalt residues, settlement of emulsions, mixtures with aggregates and density.
    • Control of inventories of raw materials and production needs depending on the formulations that were manufactured.
    • Schedule of equipment maintenance programs.
    • Direct contact by email and telephone with the Plant Manufacturer DenimoTech and Reagent Akzo Nobel to improve quality of formulations and productivity of the Asphalt plant.
    • Management and storage of waste produced in the activities, as well as the control of occupational safety in terms of handling reagents and hazardous work.
    • Training of staff in occupational safety, work assistance in production and equipment’s maintenances.
    • Performance Analysis Reports of the personnel and the production plant.

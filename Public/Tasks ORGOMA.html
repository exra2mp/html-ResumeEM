Task and functions for:

  1. Profesional Sales Representative:

    • Supply and control of Inventory of Consumable Warehouses, as well as technical support pieces for all equipment.
    • Attention to government procurement bids (MINSA, IPSA, INTA, MIFIC, Universities, CSJ and others) for equipment and maintenance
      contracts with TDR and different types of procurement’s procedures for Minor Contracts, Public Bidding,
      International Public Bidding, Direct Contracts, Organizations Contracts Like PAHO, Interamerican Development Bank (BID), World Bank, BCIE.
    • Assistance with dissatisfied customer to create strategies to keep the client captive.
    • Management of orders for consumables, parts and equipment to international suppliers of the different lines of business of the company.
    • Delivery of orders to customers with the respective verification of the quality of the pieces.
    • Development of sales strategies and post-sale monitoring.
    • Meetings with General Manager and Laboratory Managers to prepare budgets and needs for new projects.
    • Sale of maintenance contracts, qualifications and calibration of equipment for the company's customers.
    • Staff Trainings for Sales Department.
    • Management of CRM System for account monitoring and sales strategy.

  2. Technical Support:

    • Installations and Trainings of Analytical Equipment in Honduras and Costa Rica according needs of the company.
    • Follow-up of equipment failures to clients and programming of corrective visits.
    • Follow up of equipment guarantees by manufacturing defects.
    • Online remote monitoring to equipments and softwares.
    • Call Support Service for clients.
    • Schedule and follow-up of maintenance contracts, qualifications and calibration of equipment for the company's customers.
    • Staff Trainings for Technical Support Department.
    • Create manuals procedures or translate for the use of Laboratory Equipments.
